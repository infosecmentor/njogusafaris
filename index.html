Welcome to

Tom Njogu Safaris

Enjoy your Safari



Having been born in Nanyuki, right on the slopes of Mt Kenya and brought up in a farmland adjacent to Mt Kenya forest. I grew up watching and interacting with wildlife on a daily basis through out my young age. We could see animals on my way to school and back home, from elephants, Giraffes, ostriches and all plain game found in the Savanah grasslands. My curiosity on animals planted a seed in me that would come to be my Job, career, hobby and simply my lifestyle. I have been guiding people on safari for over seventeen years passionately. The curiosity never ends as every day on safari is different.
